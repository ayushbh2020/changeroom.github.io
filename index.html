Our Story

After learning about the environmental, social and economic issues that come with fast fashion, three UWaterloo students, Raymond Wu, Ayush Bhargava and Sam Minkov, became interested in how they could help. However, finding sustainable & ethical fashion which matched their style, price, and values was incredibly difficult. With the mission in mind to make shopping sustainable as easy as possible, they created ChangeRoom Marketplace in 2021.
