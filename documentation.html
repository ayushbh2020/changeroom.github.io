Setting Up ChangeRoom Shopify App

If you're new to the app, you can use this as a step to step guide to setup the app. If you are looking for more information on particular pages, feel free to click on the sections below

1. Account Page
2. Sizing Table Page
3. Publishing
4. Listings Page
5. Common Publishing Errors,
6. Reviews Page
7. Sustainable Page

Verification

Verification Step

To join our marketplace, you need to meet at least one of our sustainable requirements to ensure the integrity of our marketplace. Please submit a description of what your company does to meet the sustainability goal. You should hear back from us within 48 hours via email.

Verification Approval:

This is what it should look like when you are approved by our team. Before you are approved, you are unable to publish products, but can still setup the rest of the app in advance.

Account Page

Step 1:

Start the process by clicking connect, to link your store to ChangeRoom. Next, enter in your contact information so customers can reach you if they have any product or shipping related questions. Finally add your display address which is the website url the public can find you with for example https://changeroom.io.

Enter in a short description about your brand or your sustainability values. Add your logo using the upload button. These elements will be added to your personalized brand page on the app.

Step 2:

Next add your return policy, fulfillment times and free shipping price to give your customers some information about these processes. Press the save button on the banner so you don't lose all your changes. Before we can complete the account page, we need to head to the sizing table page, use the left navigation to do so.

Sizing Table Page

Step 3:

We need you to create sizing charts for the products that you add on the marketplace. This will help us with categorization and user sizing help. Add you first size chart by pressing add new sizing table in the top right.

Step 4:

Create a name of for the size chart, select the gender and the category the size chart belongs to. If the category isn't there for the clothing you are looking , we currently do not have that clothing category on our app. You can send a request for the category to our email - changeroomofficial@gmail.com which we can try to add in the future.

Step 5:

Add the size chart sizes by seperating sizes with commas from smallest to biggest size in the first input box. Select your desired sizing unit. Then enter the sizing details of the required body measurments for the category of clothing you choose. For better sizing recommendation accuracy, add additional measurments you have for the product. When the add measurments button is pressed, a diagram also pops up visualizing the measurments we are looking for.

After completing this step and ensuring all the information in the chart is correct, go to the top of the page and press "Publish" and then "Save" in the top right corner. If you would like to save your progress or double check the information, just press "Save" and you'll be able come back and edit/publish the chart.

Step 6:

Keep on adding size charts until you have covered all the products you would like to add to our marketplace. When you are finished making size charts, you are ready to start making products avaiable on the ChangeRoom marketplace. Head back to the account page using the left navigation.

Publishing

Step 7:

Scroll all the way down to publishing which is one of the most important parts of the app. This is where you can add products to the marketplace, there are two steps to do so. First, you need to make your products visible to ChangeRoom app by pressing manage availability.

Step 8:

In the bulk editor, you make products you want to add to our marketplace. By making products available to the ChangeRoom Marketplace, they will be visible on the Shopify app. Please note that these products don't automatically get published to the mobile app. We will go through that process in the Step 10. There is also an alternate way below to make your products available to the marketplace in the products section of your admin.

Alternatively, bulk select all your products on your store and press more actions and then add available channels. You will be able to decide what products you want to publish to the mobile app in Step 10 so don't worry about adding all your products.

Make the products available on the ChangeRoom Marketplace. These products are now visible to our Shopify app.

Step 9:

Head to your account page and scroll back down to publishing. Now press "View Product Listings" or press "Listings" on the left navigation to start publishing products to the app.

Listings Page

Step 10:

In the listings page, the only section you will find products is in the Requires View tab, as none of your products are published to the mobile app like previoulsy stated. The main error you see is the lack of a size table.

Step 11:

To make the process of adding size charts easy, feel free to apply product types by selecting the types and then add. Similarly, this can be done with tags or you can simply search for things. These allow you to find groups of products that fit a particular sizing chart.

Step 12:

To make the process of adding size charts easy, feel free to apply product types as shown or apply tags and search for things. These allow you to find groups of products that fit a particular sizing chart. Then you can select all and press the "Set Sizing Table".

Step 13:

Set Sizing Table to the correct one and repeat this process until all the products you want on the markeplace have a size chart.

Step 14:

If the product has no errors, it is moved to the ready to publish tab. Here you can select all the products you would like to publish to our mobile app by selecting and pressing "Publish to ChangeRoom Marketplace". If you notice, that a lot of the products still require review, scroll down to the Common Publishing Errors section in this documentation.

Step 15:

The products in the published section are now sucessfully on the ChangeRoom mobile app. If you would like to unpublish producs, select the produce then press "Unpublish from ChangeRoom Marketplace". However avoid this action when possible, as it lowers the user experience on the mobile app.

Common Publishing Errors

Size table is missing:

Please add a size table to the product by selecting the product adn pressing "Set Size Table"

Size table not found:

Please add a size table to the product from the sizing table column

Size table not published:

Please go to your size table page and make sure the size chart is published. There should be a blue tag signifying that its published. If there isn't, click edit the size chart, then ensure the information in the size chart is correct then press publish and save. See further information in the Sizing Page documentation

Product size options not included in size chart options:

The size chart that has been linked to this product doesn't include all the sizes in the product. For example if your product size variants are Small, Medium, Large and XLarge but the size charts are S,M,L, the size chart does not include the XL size variant which is the issue. Or if you your product variants use a different sizing system for example 40,42,44 while your size chart is S,M,L that would not work either.

To fix this issue go check the products size variants and then either create a new size chart for this product which includes all the sizing variants or edit the size chart itself. If you do choose to edit the size chart, make sure you aren't about to cause the same issues for other products that have been attached to the size chart.

Description Missing, Featured Image and all other product related errors

Please go to the product and add the required information to ensure a proper user experience for your customers

Reviews Page

Reviews Dashboard

Respond to Reviews

Respond to reviews by pressing manage and then entering a message.

Sustainable Values Page

Badges Application

This is where you'll find all the possible sustainable and ethical badges we have. Apply to as many badges as you can, by submitting an application, you will be contacted VIA email about your application in under 48 hours. These badges will be shown to you customers on our marketplace, we want to be as transparent as possible and show them the values of our brands on our marketplace. Please note that the description gets greyed after you submit an application.

Badges Written Component

After your badge is approved, it will pop into the Sustainable Values or Other Values page. It is critical you write about each value, so your customers know WHY you are labeled with the badge, to truly ensure a transparent experience.
